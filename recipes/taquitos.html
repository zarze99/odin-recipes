Chicken Taquitos Recipe

From Allrecipes: "Smoked cheese and green salsa give these crispy chicken taquitos that extra yum! Thinner corn tortillas will work the best. If you can't find the smoked cheese blend in your area, try using regular smoked Cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese."

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and shredded
  • 3/2 cups shredded cheese
  • 7 ounce green salsa
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 20 small corn torillas
  • 1 quart peanut oil

Optional:

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup chunky salsa
  • 1 cup guacamole

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 350 F
  2. Mix together the chicken, cheese, green salsa, and onion.
  3. Spoon about 3/2 tablespoons into tortillas. Secure with toothpicks
  4. Fry about 4 to 5 rolls at a time so there is even space between them while cooking. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes, until golden brown and floating.
